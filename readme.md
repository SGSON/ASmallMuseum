The purpose of the "A Small Museum" is for non-professional person to share their reviews and arts such as music and drawing to others. This application make easier to understand arts and would be boost culture.

First, users can review to a posting after apreciating. Users will write down their feeling, question and criticism without abuse. 
Second, artist can check a rating.  
Third, artists leave the their messages in the composition, so users can easily understand the meaning of the composition.
